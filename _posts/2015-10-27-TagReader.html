--- layout: post title: "The Iron Yard Final Project - TagReader" date: 2015-10-27 14:30:00 tag: featured ---

This application allows you to log on with your Pocket account and will fetch your untagged articles from your queue. As it imports the articles, the application connects to the AlchemyAPI natural language processor and attaches key concepts as tags. You can edit and then add these tags to the original article in your Pocket queue for better organization.

Tech and tools used:
Ruby on Rails, HAML, Javascript, jQuery, Semantic UI, AJAX, OmniAuth, Pocket API, AlchemyAPI, PostgreSQL, pg search
